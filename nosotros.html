About our firm

We are a team of professionals working proactively in order to search the most appropriate solutions for each case and client. In order to achieve that, we interact with various experts from other branches that complement these needs and demands.

“La conciencia es el mejor juez que tiene un hombre de bien” - Gral. José de San Martin

PRO BONO

En NFC & Abogados en concordancia con nuestros valores, asumimos el compromiso de prestar servicios legales gratuitamente que benefician a personas o comunidades menos privilegiadas, y a las organizaciones que los asisten. Estos pueden incluir la representación y asesoría en temas de interés público, que de otra manera no las obtendrían de una manera efectiva.


PRO BONO

In accordance with our values, NFC & Abogados assume the commitment to provide free legal services in order to benefit less privileged people or communities, as well as the organizations that assist them. These may include representation and counseling on topics of public interest, otherwise it would not be obtained in an effective manner.
